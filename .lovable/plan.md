

# NextGen Sustainable Wealth — Immersive Product Showcase Website

An Apple-inspired, scroll-driven product showcase website for BNP Paribas Singapore's NextGen Sustainable Wealth mobile app. Clean Anthropic-style aesthetics with white, light turquoise (#E0F5F5), and light gold (#F5E6C8) as the base palette.

---

## Page Structure (Single-Page Scroll Experience)

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-viewport cinematic hero with the app name "NextGen Sustainable Wealth" and tagline: *"Where your values meet your portfolio."*
- Animated phone mockup floating center-stage showing the portfolio dashboard screen
- Subtle particle/gradient animation in turquoise-to-gold
- "Explore the Experience" scroll-down CTA with smooth anchor

### 2. The Problem — Why This Exists
- Bold statement section: *"NextGen investors want control, transparency, and impact — not another generic dashboard."*
- Three animated stat cards fading in: portfolio fragmentation, greenwashing prevalence, ESG data gaps
- BNP Paribas Singapore competition context badge

### 3. ESG Identity Onboarding Flow (Interactive Demo)
- Phone mockup showing swipe-card onboarding screens
- Interactive carousel users can click through: value selection → ESG priority ranking → risk profiling → ESG Identity Card generation
- The ESG Identity Card renders live with sample data, showing ESG persona, top values, sophistication percentile
- Animated progress indicator

### 4. Unified Portfolio Dashboard (Hero Feature)
- Large phone mockup with the dashboard screen — holdings showing financial return AND ESG score side-by-side
- Scroll-triggered annotations highlighting: total ESG health score, daily change, multi-provider aggregation
- Interactive toggle demo: switch between "Financial View" and "ESG + Financial View" to demonstrate the core philosophy
- Callout: *"ESG is not a filter. It's a first-class data point."*

### 5. Hidden Risk Detector
- Dramatic reveal section with dark turquoise background
- Phone mockup showing flagged holdings with SGD-denominated risk estimates
- Three example risk cards animate in: water dependency risk, carbon border tax exposure, governance red flag
- Each card shows the financial impact estimate

### 6. Three-Layer ESG Scoring Engine
- Visual diagram: Claimed → Verified → Projected layers with animated divergence indicator
- Greenwashing Risk Score color-coded display (red/yellow/green)
- Interactive company example card showing all three layers and the gap score
- Multi-rater divergence flag visualization

### 7. Real-Time Preference Setting (Interactive Demo)
- Phone mockup with live toggle switches and intensity sliders
- Users can interact with filter toggles (fossil fuel exclusion, TCFD compliance, diversity threshold)
- Animated portfolio re-scoring effect when toggles change
- Preference drift detection notification mockup

### 8. Scenario Analysis & Carbon Impact
- Side-by-side dual line charts: current portfolio vs ESG-optimized under climate scenarios
- Stranded Asset vs Transition Opportunity paired view
- Carbon Tax Impact Wallet showing SGD projections for contrasting companies
- Time horizon selector (5/10/15/20 years)
- Regulatory Horizon heat map visualization

### 9. Behavioral Nudges & Cost of Inaction
- Notification-style nudge cards animating in with context triggers
- "Dismiss" interaction showing Cost of Inaction estimator popup
- Like-for-Like transition nudge with comparison view
- Gradual Shift Mode timeline visualization
- Suitability badge and "Why am I seeing this?" explainer

### 10. Impact Dashboard & Singapore Map
- Rich data visualization section with impact metrics: CO₂ avoided, water saved, clean energy supported
- Real-world equivalents (trees planted, homes powered)
- Interactive Singapore map with pinned local impact locations (Marina Bay green bond, Jurong solar, etc.)
- SDG alignment wheel chart
- Collective impact counter

### 11. Discovery & Investment Switching
- Like-for-Like Switcher comparison cards
- ESG Unlock System tier progression visualization
- Thematic Investment Explorer grid (clean energy, water, circular economy, etc.)
- GreenRewards points indicator and redemption catalogue preview

### 12. Wealth Legacy Mode
- Emotionally resonant section with generational wealth projector visualization
- Goal-setting mockup: "Net-zero aligned by 45"
- 10-year transition roadmap timeline
- Exportable impact report preview

### 13. Compliance & Trust Architecture
- Clean section showing: suitability guardrails, MAS-aligned disclosures, audit trail, conflict of interest transparency
- Regulatory compliance badges per framework (MAS, TCFD, SFDR, EU Taxonomy, SGX)
- Always-visible suitability profile concept

### 14. Navigation Logic & Information Architecture
- Animated app sitemap/flow diagram showing screen-to-screen navigation
- Tab bar: Dashboard → Discover → Impact → Profile
- Drill-down flows visualized with connecting lines

### 15. Technical Foundation
- Clean API architecture diagram showing all endpoints
- Partnership roadmap (GreenRewards 4-stage pathway)
- Future roadmap items: Institutional Alignment, Earnings Call Monitor, Collective Shareholder Engagement

### 16. Footer CTA
- *"The future of wealth is sustainable, transparent, and personal."*
- BNP Paribas Singapore branding
- Competition context

## Design System
- **Colors**: White (#FFFFFF), Light Turquoise (#E0F5F5 / #B8E6E6), Light Gold (#F5E6C8 / #E8D5A8), Dark text (#1A1A2E)
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, large bold headlines, generous whitespace (Anthropic aesthetic)
- **Animations**: Scroll-triggered fade-ins, parallax phone mockups, smooth transitions
- **Phone Mockups**: CSS-rendered iPhone frames containing app screen designs
- **Data Visualizations**: Recharts for charts, custom SVG for the Singapore map and diagrams
- **Layout**: Full-width sections alternating white and subtle tinted backgrounds
